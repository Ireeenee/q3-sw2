Disney Princesses
Disney Princess Information Symbol/s

Aurora

Movie: Sleeping Beauty 1959 | Other name/s: Briar Rose, Dornöschen

❌: Maleficent | ✅: The Three Good Fairies (Flora, Fauna, Merryweather) | 💋: Prince Phillip

Fun Facts:

1.) Aurora is the first Disney Princess film to have bows and arrows 🏹.

2.) Prince Phillip is the first proactive prince in a Disney film for killing the antagonist ⚔️.

3.) This is the last film released before Walt Disney's lifetime came to an end 💐.


Cinderella

Movie: Cinderella 1950 | Other name/s: Ella

❌: Lady Tremaine | ✅: The Fairy God Mother | 💋: Prince Charming

Fun Facts:

1.) Cinderellas's shoe size is 4.5 in women's 👠.

2.) Cinderella lost her shoe three times: (a) during breakfast, (b) at midnight, (c) after the wedding 🕛.

3.) Cinderella's iconic glass slipper symbolizes her delicateness but adaptable persona 💙.


Snow White

Movie: Snow White 1937 | Other names: n/a

❌: The Evil Queen | ✅: The Seven Dwarves (Doc, Sneezy, Grumpy, Happy, Bashful, Sleepy, Dopey) | 💋: Prince Florian

Fun Facts:

1.) Snow White has a record breaking number of first's: First film by Disney, first Disney Princess, first main character, and first heroine of a Disney animated film 🏆.

2.) Since Snow White is the first Disney Princess, her known dress uses the primary colors ❤️💙💛.

3.) Snow White's feet are smaller than Cinderella! 👠.


Mulan

Movie: Mulan 1998 | Other names: Fa Mulan

❌: Shan Yu | ✅: Mushu | 💋: n/a

Fun Fact/s:

1.) Fa Mulan is inspired by Hua Mulan 💎.

2.) Mulan is the only Disney Princess who was not of royal blood nor married royal blood 👑.

3.) Mulan was the last Disney Princess of the Disney Renaissance 🌙.


Belle

Movie: Beauty and the Beast 1991 | Other name/s: Beauty, La Belle enfant

❌: Gaston | ✅: Maurice (father) | 💋: Prince Adam (the Beast)

Fun Facts:

1.) Belle is the only Disney Princess with hazel eyes 🤎.

2.) The scene where the Prince carries Belle at the end is a reference the poster of "It's a Wonderful Life" 🔔.

3.) Being a bookworm, Some of the books she has read are Sleeping Beauty, Romeo and Juliet, and King Arthur 📖.
